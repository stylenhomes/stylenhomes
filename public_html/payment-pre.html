Payment

When operating an ecommerce platform selling interior design products, it's important to establish clear payment policies to ensure a secure and transparent transaction process for your customers. Our payment policies are as follows:

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods for purchasing interior design products on our platform:

- Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, etc.

- Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, etc.

- PayPal- Other secure payment gateways

‍

2. Payment Security:

We prioritize the security of our customers' payment information. All transactions on our platform are encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways. We do not store or have access to your complete payment details, such as credit card numbers or passwords.

‍

3. Order Confirmation:

All product prices displayed on our platform are listed in the applicable currency. Prices are subject to change without prior notice. The total order amount will include the product price(s) and any applicable taxes, shipping fees, or handling charges.

5. Sales Tax:

Sales tax or any other applicable taxes will be applied to your order based on the regulations of the jurisdiction to which the product is being shipped. The applicable tax amount will be calculated during the checkout process and included in the final order total.

‍

6. Payment Verification:

In some cases, we may need to verify your payment details before processing your order. This verification process is conducted to prevent fraudulent transactions and protect both our customers and our business. We may request additional information or documentation to complete the payment verification process.

‍

7. Payment Authorization and Capture:

When you place an order, the payment will be authorized but not immediately captured. Payment capture occurs once your order has been processed and prepared for shipment. If there are any issues with payment authorization or capture, we will contact you to resolve the matter.

‍

8. Order Cancellation and Refunds:

If you need to cancel your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. Orders can only be canceled before they have been processed for shipment. Refunds for canceled orders or eligible returns will be issued in accordance with our Returns and Refunds Policy, using the original payment method whenever possible.

‍

9. Payment Disputes:

In the event of a payment dispute or suspected fraudulent activity, we reserve the right to suspend or cancel the order until the issue is resolved. We may request additional information or collaborate with the payment processor to investigate the matter and protect the interests of both parties involved.

‍

10. Currency Conversion:

For international orders, currency conversion may be required if the transaction is processed in a currency different from the currency of the customer's payment method. Currency conversion rates are determined by the payment processor or financial institution at the time of the transaction.
