Shipping

At Style & Homes, we strive to provide a seamless and reliable shipping experience for our customers when purchasing interior design products. Please review the following shipping policies that govern the delivery of your orders:

‍

1. Shipping Methods and Carriers:

We offer standard shipping for all orders, fulfilled through reputable shipping carriers such as FedEx, UPS, or DHL.

‍

2. Shipping Zones and Delivery Areas:

We currently offer shipping to customers within the continental United States. Unfortunately, we do not ship internationally at this time.

‍

3. Processing Time:

Once your order is placed and payment is confirmed, we require a processing time of [X] business days to prepare your order for shipment. Please note that this processing time does not include weekends or holidays.

‍

4. Shipping Costs:

Shipping costs are calculated based on the total weight of the order and the destination address. The shipping cost will be displayed during the checkout process before you complete your purchase.

‍

5. Delivery Timeframes:

Estimated delivery timeframes vary depending on the destination address and the shipping method selected during checkout. On average, you can expect your order to arrive within 10 to 15 business days after it has been shipped. Please note that these timeframes are estimates and may be subject to unforeseen delays, such as weather conditions or carrier disruptions.

‍

6. Tracking and Order Status:

Once your order has been shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your shipment. Additionally, you can log in to your account on our website to view the order status and track your package directly from there.

‍

7. Shipping Notifications and Communication:

We will send you email notifications at various stages of the shipping process, including when your order is confirmed, when it has been shipped, and when it is out for delivery. If there are any significant delays or issues with your shipment, we will make our best effort to communicate these to you promptly.

‍

8. Packaging and Handling:

o ensure the safe delivery of your interior design products, we take great care in packaging them securely. Fragile items will be adequately protected with appropriate packaging materials to minimize the risk of damage during transit.

‍

9. Multiple Items or Split Shipments:

If your order includes multiple items, we may ship them together in one shipment or separately, depending on factors such as item availability and size. You will be notified during the checkout process if your order will be split into multiple shipments. Rest assured, you will not incur any additional shipping charges for split shipments.

‍

10. Returns and Exchanges:

For information regarding returns and exchanges related to shipping, please refer to our comprehensive Returns and Exchanges Policy. It provides detailed instructions on how to initiate a return or exchange and any associated shipping procedures.

‍

11. Disclaimers:

While we make every effort to ensure timely delivery, please note that we are not responsible for any delays, damages, or loss caused by circumstances beyond our control, such as natural disasters, carrier errors, or incorrect shipping information provided by the customer.

‍

If you have any questions or require further assistance regarding shipping, please feel free to reach out to our customer support team, and we will be happy to assist you.
