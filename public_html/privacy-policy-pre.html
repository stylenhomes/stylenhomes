PRIVACY POLICY

We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our ecommerce platform to purchase interior design products. By accessing and using our platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

‍

1. Information We Collect:

a. Personal Information: When you place an order or create an account on our platform, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping address, billing address, and contact information.

b. Payment Information: To process your payment, we collect payment details such as credit card information, billing address, and other payment-related data. However, please note that we do not store or have access to complete payment details, as they are handled securely by our trusted payment processors.

c. Communication Information: We may collect information related to your communication with us, including emails, customer support inquiries, and feedback.

‍

2. Use of Personal Information:

a. Order Processing and Fulfillment: We use your personal information to process and fulfill your orders, including shipping the products to the provided address and communicating order-related information.

b. Customer Support: We may use your personal information to respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and address any issues or concerns you may have.

c. Marketing Communications: With your consent, we may send you promotional emails or newsletters about our products, special offers, or updates. You can opt-out of these communications at any time.

d. Legal Obligations: We may use or disclose your personal information as required by law, regulations, legal proceedings, or governmental requests.

‍

3. Protection of Personal Information:

a. Data Security: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

b. Data Retention: We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

‍

4. Sharing of Personal Information:

a. Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, processing payments, shipping products, or providing other services. These service providers have access to personal information solely for the purpose of performing their duties and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

b. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your personal information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes, or to respond to lawful requests from public authorities.

‍

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze usage patterns, and personalize content. You have the option to disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that certain features of our platform may not function properly without cookies.

‍

6. Children's Privacy:

Our platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information on our platform, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove such information.

‍

7. Updates to the Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our privacy practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this policy regularly. Continued use of our platform after any changes to the Privacy Policy constitutes your consent to the revised policy.

‍

8. Contact Us:

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact our designated privacy officer at [info@stylenhomes.com].
